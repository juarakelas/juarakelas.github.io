A descriptive sentence is a sentence that describes something so that when you read it, you feel as if you directly feel what is written.

The word that describes American society in 1922 is "Do not attract attention to yourself in public". In the text, it is told how the norms prevailed in American society in 1922. Where maintaining the safety of oneself and others is something that must be upheld
