1. Contoh cerita yang menggunakan Past Tense dan Present Perfect


Berikut ini adalah satu contoh cerita dengan menggunakan past tense dan present perfect.

The head coach of Barcelona, Xavi called up his players the day after Blaugrana's devastating loss at home while playing host to Cadiz.

"Lads, what on earth was going up there?  Did you realise what it cost for our defeat on the weekend? Look, I know our team is not in their ideal best but I expected you to make a positive response there."

"Coach, my apology, we were exhausted and just unlucky to break the deadlock.", said Barca's new signings, Auba.

"Yes, coach, I knew we lost our chance for stopping Madrid from snatching the silverware early but we were just unfortunate.", responded Ousmane.

"Sorry, lads, but you are in FC Barcelona. Unlucky is not the reason. This is the elite and if you wanna be apart of the elite, you must prepare to suffer for the high demands here.", replied Xavi.

"Fellows, our coach is right. OK we lost against Frankfurt and last weekend versus Cadiz. Yet, in Barcelona, we can not afford to lose again. If you want to be apart of Blaugrana's history, we should bounce back in San Sebastian later.", added the captain, Gerard.

They then started their Monday training session and Xavi talked to his assistant about who shall be in the line up. He then had a conversation with Gerard regarding his injury.

Finally, the D-day has come. The team left Camp Nou early morning to San Sebastian and immediately did the final session of the tactical training on Thursday afternoon. Xavi then met his team before getting ready.

"OK, lads, we've come this far, made a great progress this year and beaten Madrid in Bernabeu. Now go out there and show Sociedad who we really are. Vamos!!!", said the coach.

"Vamos!!!", replied his men.

Penjelasan:

Cerita yang menggunakan past tense dan present perfect adalah jenis cerita yang mengandung peristiwa masa lampau dan kegiatan, pencapaian atau hal yang telah dilakukan. Maka dari itu kisah tentang sebuah pengalaman atau pencapaian seseorang adalah yang paling pas.

Berikut ini adalah pola dari dua tenses tersebut.

Past tense: S + Verb 2 + O + complementPresent perfect tense: S + have/has + V3 + complement.

Beberapa contohnya dapat dilihat pada contoh cerita yang dicetak tebal (untuk past tense) dan tebal miring (untuk present perfect).

Jawaban:

Present Tense

There are six people in my family: my father, mother, three daughters and one grandmother. I am a third daughter with two elder sisters. My first elder sister is Natashia; another sister’s name is Nandiya.

My father is the most handsome man because there is no son in my family. He works as a taxi driver. He loves helping people and meeting new people. He always smiles and thanks to God for everything. He works very hard to fulfill all of our needs.

My mother is also a hard worker. Every morning she wakes up so early to cook food then sell it. She is the best chef for us. We love her cooking. We usually help her to prepare food to sell. Mother never forgets to care all people in our family.

We also live with our grandmother. She is our father’s mother. We love our grandmother. Sometimes she is talkative but we know that she love us. She loves gardening. She plants many flowers in our garden. The flowers make our house beautiful.

Present Continuous Tense

Today, Chiya is visiting her grandparents. She loves cooking with her grandmother. Her grandma usually teaches her how to cook delicious dishes. At this moment, Chiya is learning how to make tasty chicken soup.

Chiya is helping to cut some onions, chilies, carrots, potatoes and other vegetables. She is also writing her grandmother’s recipe. She plans to make chicken soup at home next Monday. She is making the chicken soup for her mother. Next Monday is her mother’s birthday.

An hour later, Chiya and grandparents have finished eating chicken soup. Now, Chiya is listening to her grandfather’s story. Chiya also loves listening to her grandpa’s story.

Her gandpa has many stories about life, joke, culture and many more. Now, Chiya with grandmother are listening to one story of The Arabian Nights (1001 nights). They’re enjoying their time together.

Present Perfect Tense

This is about my younger sister, Leenda. She is a High School student. She has always imagined herself as a musician. She has told me about her dream many times. It’s been her dream since elementary school.

Who has inspired her to be a musician? A great singer named Whitney Houston has been her favorite singer. Leenda has always listened to Whitney’s songs many times in a day. She loves the song entitled “I have nothing” by Whitney Houston.

Leenda has also learned how to play a guitar for a year. She has made one Youtube channel since 2017. She has covered many songs and uploaded in her channel. Many viewers have written positive comments about her covers.

Sometimes Leenda has music performances with her friends. Leenda and her friends have just done a music performance this month. They are also great players. They have won five music competitions this year. They all want to be great musicians.

Present Perfect Continuous Tense

Adi has been living in Surabaya for all his life. He has a book. He has had that books for three months. Now, he wants to read. Adi reads book in a room. Adi has a friend. Her name is Anna. She is not from Surabaya. She has been living in Surabaya since 2012.

His mother has cooked and his father is taking a bath when Anna calls him. She wants to play with him. Anna asks Adi, “Do you want to play with me?”

Adi answers, “Ok. Come here. I have finished reading a good book and I am watching TV with my mom.”

Adi and Anna play together. After hours, Adi asks Anna, “Have you eaten already?”

Anna replies, “No, I haven’t. I am hungry.”

At last, they go to kitchen for eating. Adi does not like chicken. He eats vegetables. Anna eats chicken but she does not like vegetables. They do not like eggs.

Adi’s mother comes and says, “What are you doing here? Don’t eat here.“

Adi and Anna go to dining room.

12. Contoh cerita bahasa inggris yang didalam 1 cerita terdapat simple past tense, present perfect tense, past perfect tense


Haunted theatre
Last week, me and my family went to XXI in Kuningan city mall of Jakarta. When we arrived at the mall, we directly rushed to XXI cinema and bought the tickets. After we bought the tickets, we went to have lunch. We had our lunch in Ichiban sushi. We checked the time and it's our turn to watch so we decided to hurry and go to the cinema. When we arrived, the cinema was very deserted and quiet. Then we finally realized that there was no one. Not a single soul except the silent waiter, the ticket cashier and us. The atmosphere was really quiet and my mother was scared so we entered the the studio quickly. Inside the studio, there was no one again. I started to feel the chills and cringed. Not long later the lamps was turned off and the movie began. During the movie, I felt something or someone touched me. I was curious so I looked behind. I saw a shadow of a man without the person. I thought my minds was playing tricks with me so i just forget about the shadow and enjoyed the movie. After the movie finished, I asked my mother and father whether they had felt someone touched them. They said that they also felt the same. Suddenly, a nearby lamp explodes by itself! Me and my family was really shocked and ran away to the parking lot. We rushed home as fast as we can. At home, I did a research about the cinema and found out that there was a person called Mr. Jenovocic who died during the fire in the cinema. When I read the review, people said that his shadow and soul never left the cinema and always appear on 23rd May, the day he died. I also found out that 23rd May was the day when I and my family went there. I faced left and right to check if there are any shadows. Fortunately, there are no shadows. But, I forgot to face behind. When I faced behind I saw a shadow and went into a coma for two whole days. When I woke up I realized that shadow was my own shadow.

15. cerita tentang pengalaman yang menggunakan kalimat present tense ,past tense,past perfect dan present perfecf​


Present tense digunakan untuk menjelaskan apa yang kamu lakukan (sering lakukan).

Rumus : S + V1 + O

Contoh : I clean the class every day

Past tense digunakan untuk menjelaskan apa yang sudah terjadi di masa lampau (dulu).

Rumus : S + V2 + O

Contoh : I cleaned the class yesterday

Past perfect digunakan untuk menjelaskan apa yang telah kamu lakukan.

Rumus : S + Had + V3 + O

Present perfect

Rumus : S + Have + V3 + O
