8. ceritakan pengalaman saat libur akhir semester satu di rumah dalam bahasa inggris harus ada terjemahannya


Yogyakarta, 26th December 2016

26th December has come, it's time for my school holiday. I've been waiting for this day to come. Well, my family and I haven't planned on going somewhere yet to spend our holiday. Hmm.. maybe not, we're not going somewhere this holiday, because I want to spend my holiday at home. I want to feel how's it feel to be at home when a long holiday comes. After I talked to my parents, they agreed, I was happy to hear that. The second day has come, and I started to feel bored, I had an idea, "How about inviting my best friend to come over so we can play together? Hmm... let me try to invite him." I brang up my phone, and started to text my friend. "Hey, I feel bored at home, could you please come over if you can? I have something to play with you if you want to." 2 minutes later, my friend replied, "Uh.. I'm sorry, but I'm on vacation right now, I guess I can't come to your house right now, sorry..." After I read that reply, I feel dissapointed, but I can understand, because this is a school holiday, people are usually on vacation. "Huh.. I guess I'll just watch TV and play my favorite game." After I satisfied on playing games and watching TV, I looked at the clock, and I was surprised, "Huh?! 10 O'clock?! Wow that was quick! I guess I'll be going to bed now." 3 days have passed, I did the same thing on those 3 days, but this time it's different. I've got a surprise from my parents. "Happy Birthday!", said my parents. "Oh God, thank you so much guys!". "We've got a present for you, we hope you like it." 
After opening the present, I got shocked. "PS4?! Oh my God! Thank you so much! I love you guys!". "We're glad you liked it, now you can spend the rest of your school holiday by playing with your present. Hope you doesn't get bored again..". "Of course I won't! This is going to be awesome!". After I thanked my parents, I quickly set things up, and boot it up. Then I inserted a game disk, and started playing it (my parents also gave me 3 games, and those are my favoite games). (1 day later, 31st December) It's 23:59 now, It's time to prepare things up to celebrate New Year. I can wait to light up the fireworks to celebrate it. Oh God this is it.. 5, 4, 3, 2, 1.. Happy New Year!! *lighted up the fireworks*. "Wow, this is the best New Year celebration ever! Those fireworks are very beautiful! I can't forget this moment!". I also said a wish, " I wish I can be more successful, and I wish I can change to a better person this year. Thanks for everything 2016.." (4 days later) "Wow, 4th January already. Well this is good, because I can finally meet my friends again at school. Well, better prepare things up for school tomorrow.."
That's my story, hope you can understand it :)

13. Tolong buatkan teks recount tentang pengalaman liburan dalam bahasa Inggris beserta terjemahannya dan sertakan dengan judul


Jawaban:

My First Time In Yogyakarta

My family and I went to my grandmother’s house in Yogyakarta last month. It was my first trip to this city. We went there two days after my sister’s gradution ceremony in Semarang. We arrived at Yogyakarta at night. We spent a week staying in my grandmother’s house which is 5 minutes away by foot to Malioboro street.

In the first morning, we were still too tired after a long trip from Semarang to Yogyakarta. So we decided to stay at home to recharge our energy. I walk around the neighborhood with my sister just to experience how it is like to be in Yogyakarta. There were too many house, I think, which made the space between a house and the other was so small, even the road was also small that only bicycle and motorcycle can go through.

On the second day, all of us went to Malioboro street. We saw so many merchant with various of product which they claim to be a traditional product of Yogyakarta. I bought some wooden figurine and T-shirt with the word “Yogyakarta” printed on it, while my sister bought some leather handbag. My mom and dad were busy choosing some merchandise to be brought home when we go back.

On the third day, we went to Taman Sari and Keraton Ngayogyakarta Hadiningrat to see some historical building in Yogyakarta. We took a lot of picture there. We also took some picture of the building so we can check it again at home. We found some place providing Yogya traditional food around the building and we jumped in right away.

We spent the rest of our week in Yogyakarta by visiting some Shopping Malls such as Jogja City Mall, Malioboro Mall, Hartono Mall and Ambarrukmo Plaza. We realized that Yogyakarta turned out to be very warm during the day, that was the reason why we decided to spend more time in air conditioned building like this.

Terjemahannya:

Kali Pertama Saya Ke Yogyakarta

Keluarga saya dan saya pergi ke rumah nenek saya di Yogyakarta bulan lalu. Itu merupakan perjalanan pertama saya ke kota ini. Kami pergi kesana dua hari setelah upacara wisuda kakak saya di Semarang. Kami sampai di Yogyakarta pada malam hari. Kami menghabiskan satu minggu tinggal di rumah nenek saya yang berjarak 5 menit jalan kaki dari jalan Malioboro.

Pada pagi hari pertama, kami masih terlalu lelah setelah perjalanan panjang dari Semarang ke Yogyakarta. Jadi kami memutuskan untuk tetap di rumah untuk mengisi tenaga kami. Saya berjalan jalan di lingkungan rumah bersama kakak saya untuk merasakan bagaimana rasanya berada di Yogyakarta. Terdapat terlalu banyak rumah, menurut saya, yang membuat jarak antara satu rumah dengan rumah lainnya begitu sempit, bahkan jalanan nya juga kecil hingga hanya sepeda dan sepeda motor yang bisa lewat.

Pada hari kedua, kami semua pergi ke jalan Malioboro. Kami melihat ada begitu banyak pedagang dengan berbagai macam produk yang mereka akui sebagai produk tradisional dari Yogyakarta. Saya membeli beberapa patung kayu dan kaos oblong dengan tulisan “Yogyakarta” tercetak di atasnya, sementara itu kakak saya membeli beberapa tas tangan kulit. Ibu dan Ayah saya sibuk memilih beberapa barang dagangan untuk dibawa kerumah saat kami pulang.

Pada hari ketiga, kami pergi ke Taman Sari dan Keraton Ngayogyakarta Hadiningrat untuk melihat beberapa bangunan bersejarah di Yogyakarta. Kami mengambil banyak poto di sana. Kami juga mengambil beberapa poto dari bangunan itu supaya kami bisa melihatnya lagi di rumah. Kami menemukan beberapa tembat yang menyediakan makanan khas Yogya disekitar bangunan itu dan kami langsung saja masuk kesana.

Kami menghabiskan sisa minggu kami di Yogyakarta dengan mengunjungi beberapa pusat perbelanjaan seperti Jogja City Mall, Malioboro Mall, Hartono Mall dan Ambarrukmo Plaza. Kami menyadari bahwa ternyata Yogyakarta sangat panas pada siang hari, inilah alasannya mengapa kami memutuskan untuk menghabiskan lebih banyak waktu di dalam bangunan ber AC seperti ini.

14. Ceritakan pengalaman liburan di rumah saja dalam bahasa inggris dan terjemahan b, indonesia


Pengalaman liburan di rumah termasuk dalam jenis recount text. Jadi, untuk menceritakan pengalaman liburan di rumah yang dialami oleh penulisnya menggunakan past tense.

Pembahasan

Contoh cerita pengalaman liburan di rumah dengan menggunakan bahasa Inggris yaitu

         I spent my school holiday at home last year. I didn't go anywhere because of the rain. I spent my time in front of television or read books. My mother bought me some books. She said that it was better to read books than play game online.

         I usually woke up without alarm and took a bath when my mother asked me. After that, I played with my cat, watched television, or read a book. I also helped my mother, such as washed the dishes, swept the floor, or washed the clothes. It looked so bored. But I enjoyed my time very much. It was the best time of mine, because I hadn't to do my homework or study.

Artinya:

         Saya menghabiskan liburan sekolah saya di rumah tahun kemarin. Saya tidak pergi kemana-mana karena hujan. Saya menghabiskan liburan saya di depan TV atau membaca buku. Ibu saya membelikan beberapa buku. Ia berkata bahwa lebih baik membaca buku dari bermain gim daring.

         Saya biasanya bangun tanpa alarm dan mandi ketika ibu menyuruh saya. Setelah itu, saya bermain dengan kucing saya, menonton TV, atau membaca buku. Saya juga membantu ibu saya, seperti mencuci peralatan makan, menyapu lantai, atau mencuci baju. Tampak sangat membosankan. Tetapi saya amat menikmati waktu saya. Ini adalah waktu terbaik milik saya, karena saya tidak harus mengerjakan PR atau belajar.
