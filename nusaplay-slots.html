Passive: Users are allowed by expansions slot

10. the front side bus carries all data

Passive: All data is carried by the front aide bus

RUMUS PASSIVE VOICE

* Simple Present :

Active: S + V1 (-s/es) + O/C

Passive: S + is/am/are + V3 + by (agent)

* Simpe Past :

Active: S + V2 + O/C

Passive: S + was/were + V3 + by (agent)

Catatan:

Perubahan dari Active ke Passive dengan cara menukar posisi antara Subject dan Object dan gunakan ToBe + verb 3
