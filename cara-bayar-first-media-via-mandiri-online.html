12. bought- via- masks- doni- yesterday- online​


Jawaban:

Doni bought masks via online yesterday.

Pembahasan:

Simple past tense

Simple past tense digunakan utk menyatakan kejadian masa lampau.

Rumus:

(+) S + V2 + O

(-) S + did not + V1 + O

(?) Did + S + V1 + O + ?

Contohnyaa..

(+) I met my friend at the market yesterday.

(-) I did not meet my friend at the market yesterday.

(?) Did I meet my friend at the market yesterday?

(+) He sold his house last week.

(-) He did not sell his house last week.

(?) Did he sell his house last week?
