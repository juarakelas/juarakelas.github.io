7. Contoh undangan formal dan informal tentang festival kuliner menggunakan bahasa inggris


Formal invitation                                                                                                                        

                                                                                                  June 20, 2013


123 Sesame Street,

Orchard, Singapore 12345


Dear Mr. Stark,


On behalf of the Singapore Tourism Board, I would like to extend a warm invitation to you to attend the opening of the 1st Asian Culinary Festival on Saturday 16 October 2018. It would be a honor to have you serve as a VIP guest for the official opening ceremony.

The festival begins at 6pm at the Orchard Road, and the declaration ceremony is being held at the main hall. The festival will be opened officially by Prime Minister of Singapore.

Additional information:

Your personal tickets for attending the official opening party and location map are included

You have a possibility to invite one person to accompany you

Casual attire requested

Please reserve or contact us by telephone during office hours at +65 156 456445 or at asianculinaryfestival@sg.gov . We will follow up this invitation with a call in one week before the beginning of the festival. We hope that you will be able to join us for this event.


Yours Sincerely,

Mr. Liah Ong

Director of Singapore Tourism Board
