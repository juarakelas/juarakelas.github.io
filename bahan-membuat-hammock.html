1. Kalimat Dari kata varieties, glows, content, hammock, hatch, gambol, darken makasih


Answer: 

To make a sentence from certain words or vocabulary, you need to get the meaning of them. Here's the sentences:
- there are several varieties of flowers used for the research. (meaning: kind)
- The content of your flashdrive seems to corrupted. (meaning: what is inside)
- I can see the glow in her eyes. (meaning: something shines)
- you can darken the shades there. (make it dark)
- The campers are still sleeping in their hammock. (hanging bed for camping)
- The hatch on the spaceship is still intact. (small door on the big vehicle)
- Don't just gambol around on the street, mate. (move playfully)
