2. Berikan contoh kalimat positive negative interrogative! (7)


Berikut contoh kalimat positive, negative, dan interrogative dengan menggunakan simple present tense.

(+) She visits her grandmother every week

(-) She does not visit her grandmother every week

(?) Does she visit her grandmother every week?

(+) My sister is a doctor

(-) My sister is not a doctor

(?) Is my sister a doctor?

(+) They borrow some books from the library

(-) They do not borrow some books from the library

(?) Do they borrow some books from the library?

(+) Mr. John is an English teacher

(-) Mr. John is not an English teacher

(?) Is Mr. John is an English teacher?

(+) We sing a song for our teacher

(-) We do not sing a song for our teacher

(?) Do we sing a song for our teacher?

(+) I am a student

(-) I am not a student

(?) Am I a student?

(+) You bring a glass of water for me

(-) You do not bring a glass of water for me

(?) Do you bring a glass of water for me?

Penjelasan:

Simple present tense merupakan suatu jenis tense atau bentuk kalimat yang digunakan untuk menyatakan peristiwa yang terjadi pada saat ini (present). Selain itu, simple present tense juga digunakan untuk menyatakan kegiatan yang berlangsung secara rutin atau kebiasaan dan juga menyatakan fakta yang diketahui semua orang seperti ayam bertelur.

Struktur Kalimat

1. Kalimat verbal, jenis kalimat yang menggunakan kata kerja sebagai predikat

(+) S + V1 (+ s/es) + O

(-) S + do/ does + not + V1 + O

(?) Do/ does + S + V1 + O + ?

Contoh:

(+) She goes to the library

(-) She does not go to the library

(?) Does she go to the library?

2. Kalimat Nominal, jenis kalimat yang tidak menggunakan kata kerja sebagai predikat.

(+) S + to be (am/ is/ are) + O

(-) S + to be (am/ is/ are) + not + O

(?) To be (am/ is/ are) + S + O + ?

Contoh:

(+) She is a dancer

(-) She is not a dancer

(?) Is she a dancer?

Semoga membantu.
