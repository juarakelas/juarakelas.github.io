35 unit

40 unit

70 unit

100 unit

120 unit

Jawaban:

BEP unit = Fixed Cost / (Price sales - Variable cost)

BEP unit = 3.500.000 / (150.000 - 50.000)

BEP unit = 3.500.000 / 100.000

BEP unit = 35 unit
