1. Tuliskan cara tutorial hijab memakai bahasa inggris min 9 step


1. Place a bandana on first. This helps to keep your headscarf in place.
2. Fold the headscarf as you have been shown how to. Place it on your head.
3. Make one part up to your waist, and one part to your belly.
4. Bring the long part up over the short part and wrap it around your head.
5. Pull the short part. This will tighten it as firmly as you like.
6. Pin the wrapped section in place.
7. Leave the shorter part loose. This is used for coverage; pin in place as needed (optional).
8. Give some accessories (ig. pin or brooch) as you like.
9. Make sure your hijab is match with you.

4. how to use a hijab pashmina simple!


1.First of all use your ciput or ninja first as the basis for the hijab to be more presentable.
2.Then use your cotton pashmina headscarf by dividing the two sides, one short side and one side length (free where one is short, according to your taste)
3.Embed your hijab under your chin using pin or temiti needles (as per your convenience)
4.Then the short side (left) you bring to the right shoulder, tuck it into your shirt and trim.
5.And for the long side (right) you bring up your head until the position is on the right.
6.Pin it over your head using a pin needle to keep it going and comfortable.
7.Finish, trim the hijab you use.

1. C. 35 : 45 : 36

2. B. 35 : 37

3. A. 5

4. D. 15,0

PembahasanPembahasan no. 1

Jika a : b  = 7  : 9 dan b : c = 5 : 4 maka a : b : c adalah

a : b = 7 : 9 (kalikan 5) a : b = 35 : 45

b : c = 5 : 4 (kalikan 9) b : c = 45 : 36

Sesama b harus sama nilainya dengan cara mencari KPK

Perbandingan a : b : c = 35 : 45 : 36

Pembahasan no. 2

Jika (a + b) : (a - b) = 7 : 5 maka (a² - b²) : (a² + b²) sama dengan

a + b = 7

a – b = 5 _

_______

2b = 2

b = 2/2 = 1

a + 1 = 7

a = 6

(a² – b²)/(a² + b²)

= 36 – 1/36 + 1

= 35 : 37

Pembahasan no. 3

Nilai n yang memenuhi perbandingan senilai (n - 3) : (n - 2) = (n + 1) : (n + 4) adalah

n – 3/n – 2 = n + 1/n + 4

(n – 3)(n + 4) = (n – 2)(n + 1)

n² + n – 12 = n² - n - 2

2n  = -2 + 12

2n = 10

n = 5

Pembahasan no. 4

Jika (2n – 5) : 20 berbalik nilai dengan 12 : 15, maka nilai n sama dengan

2n – 5/20 = 12/15

2n – 5/20 = 15/12 (karena berbalik nilai, jadi posisi a2 ditaruh ke b2)

(2n – 5).12 = 20.15

24n – 60 = 300

24n = 300

n = 15
