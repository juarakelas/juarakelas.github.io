1. contoh procedure text beserta terjemahannya


How To Make Mango Juice

Materials:
A glass of ice pack
A spoon of sugar
A piece of mango
A half glass of water

Steps:
First, peel the mango and clean it
Next, cut the mango into pieces and put them into the juicer
Then, put the water, ice and sugar
After that turn on the juice and wait about 15 seconds
Finally, pour the mango juice into the glass and ready to drink

Terjemahan:
Cara Membuat mangga Juice
Bahan:
Segelas es
Sebuah sendok gula
Sepotong mangga
Setengah gelas air

Langkah langkah:
Pertama, mengupas mangga dan membersihkannya
Berikutnya, potong mangga menjadi potongan-potongan dan menempatkan mereka ke dalam juicer
Kemudian, menempatkan air, es dan gula
Setelah itu hidupkan jus dan tunggu sekitar 15 detik
Akhirnya, tuangkan jus mangga ke dalam gelas dan siap untuk diminum

5. contoh Procedure text about how to make cheese Banana dalam bahasa inggris dan terjemahannya


Goal : To Make a Cheese Banana
Tujuan : Untuk membuat Pisang Keju

Ingredients / Materials ( Bahan - bahan )
6 medium-sized bananas, peeled
( 6 pisang berukuran sedang yang sudah dikupas )
3 tbsp rice flour
( 3 sendok teh tepung beras )
3 tbsp wheat flour
( 3 sendok teh tepung gandum )
1/2 tsp vanilla essence
( 1/2 sendok teh perasa vanilla )
2 tbsp milk creamer
( 2 sendok teh krimer susu )
Grated cheese
( Keju yang sudah diparut )
Oil for deep-frying
( Minyak untuk menggoreng )
A pinch of salt
( Sejumput garam )
A pinch of sugar
( Sejumput gula )

Steps ( Langkah - langkah ) :
1.First, mix sugar, rice flour and wheat flour with water.
( Pertama, campur gula, tepung beras dan tepung gandum dengan air )
2.Then soak bananas in the batter. Heat the oil in a large wok.
( Kemudian, rendam pisang dalam mentega. Panaskan api di wajan yang besar )
3.Then drop one banana at a time into the oil. Cook only two bananas at a time as the temperature of the oil will be drop if there are too many bananas.
( Kemudian, masukkan satu persatu pisang ke dalam minyak. Masak cukup dua pisang saja dalam satu waktu karena kalau ada terlalu banyak pisang, suhu minyaknya akan menurun drastis )
4.When bananas are crisp and golden brown, remove from oil and drain on a paper towel and put aside.
( Ketika pisangnya sudah garing dan berwarna cokelat keemasan, angkat dari minyak, keringkan di tisu dan letakkan di tempat lain )
5.Pour the milk creamer on top of the fried bananas.
( Tuang krimer susu di atas pisang yang sudah masak )
6.Finally, coat it with cheese.
( Terakhir, lapisi pisang itu dengan keju )

“How to Insert Sim Card Cellphone”

Cellphone is a modern communication device which connects one to the others by voice, written message and data. However this device can not work until the SIM card is inserted.

When inserting the SIM Card to cellphone, make sure that the cellphone has been switched off and follow the direction bellow :

First of all, press the locking catch and slide the cover then lift it off the phone.

After that, push two catches in the opposite directions and remove the battery.

Next, slide the SIM card carefully into the slot and make sure that the golden connect arson are facing to the connector of the phone.

Then, put the battery and align it until snaps into its place.

Finally, insert the two catches of the back cover corresponding slot in the phone and slide the cover forward button of the phone until locks into place

Don’t forget to switch on the cellphone. Wait until it is ready to use

10. pengertian procedure text dalam bahasa inggris dan terjemahannya


Procedure text is a text that explains or helps us how to make or use something. Its social function is to describehow something is completely done through a sequence of series. Communicative purpose of this text is to describe how something is made through a sequence of actions or steps.


Teks prosedur adalah teks yang menjelaskan atau membantu kita bagaimana membuat atau menggunakan sesuatu. Fungsi sosialnya adalah menggambarkan sesuatu yang benar-benar dilakukan melalui serangkaian urutan. Tujuan komunikatif teks ini adalah untuk menggambarkan bagaimana sesuatu dibuat melalui serangkaian tindakan atau langkah.

12. procedure text tentang makanan beserta terjemahan


"How to make Pancake"

- 3 – 4 spoonful of flour
- 2 eggs
- 1 ½ (250 ml) of milk
- 1 stick of butter

MATERIALS :
- 1 mixing bowl
- 2 table spoons
- 2 cups
- 1 small pan

PROCEDURE :
1. Put the flour in the bowl.
2. Put milk in a cup.
3. Make sure it's 250 ml of milk.
4. Put the milk in the bowl.
5. Break the 2 eggs into the bowl.
6. Mix it with a spoon.
7. Heat up the pan and put the butter
8. Put the mix in the pan.
9. Let the pancake mix cook about 5 minutes
10. Flip pancake over when the top is brown.
11. Your pancake its ready to be serve!
