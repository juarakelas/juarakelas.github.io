South Jakarta

In the last vacation, I went to the grandmother's house in Jakarta. My grandmother lived in his house along with his grandfather. I always visit once a month. Her house is located in the southern Jakarta is not too far from where I live. When I arrived there, I was invited to visit the famous places in the city. My grandmother recommended so many interesting places, and made my list of places to be visited.The first place I visited was Ragunan Zoo. It is one place in South Jakarta. I shared my grandmother and grandfather by car to go there. When I arrived there, I was amazed by the views of animals which are so nice. Animal animals there is quite interesting, ranging from reptiles to wild animals such as lion and so forth. Many people who have known this place, so the conditions around Ragunan looked well maintained. After visiting Ragunan Zoo, I chose the National Monument (Monas) as the second destination. I spend a lot of money just to visit this place. But, proverbial British "Price does not lie" is true. I am really very happy to be able to hang out with Grandpa and Grandma to seethe city atmosphere.The next day, I remember my mother's orders. He asked me to buy clothes from Jakarta. So, my grandfather told me about a good place to buy good clothes and cheap.He recommended the White Brick Market ( Pasar Bata Putih ) as my next destination. So, I went to that market. There are so many people who sell different variants of clothes. I tried to bargain with the seller for a good price. Finally, I found a cheap price and buy so many clothes for my mother.After buying clothes, I went to my grandmother's house. I took a break and have lunch there. Then, I packed my luggage and say goodbye to my grandmother and grandfather. I promised to visit again next month.

Ketika saya tiba di sana, saya sangat kagum dengan pemandangan hewan hewan yang begitu bagus. Hewan hewan disana cukup menarik,mulai dari reptil hingga hewan liar seperti Singa dan lain sebagainya. Banyak orang yang telah mengetahui tempat ini, sehingga kondisi di sekitar Ragunan tampakterawat dengan baik.Setelah mengunjungi Ragunan, saya memilih Monumen Nasional (Monas) sebagai tujuan kedua. Saya menghabiskan banyak uang hanya untuk mengunjungi tempat ini. Tapi, pepatah Inggris "Harga tidak berbohong" benar. Saya sungguh sangat senang bisa berjalan jalan bersama dengan Kakek dan Nenek untuk melihat suasana kota Jakarta.Pada hari berikutnya, saya ingat pesanan ibuku. Kemudian, aku dikemas bagasi saya dan mengucapkan selamat tinggal kepada nenek dan kakek saya. Aku berjanji akan mengunjunginya bulan depan lagi.
